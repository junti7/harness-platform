# Competitive Landscape
# Version: 2.0
# Date: 2026-05-10

---

## 1. Benchmark Category

Harness should be compared first against creator-intelligence publications, not enterprise data platforms.

Reference models:

- Stratechery-style solo analyst subscription
- SemiAnalysis-style deep technical intelligence
- Doomberg-style niche paid newsletter
- Korean tech newsletters and paid creator products

Enterprise platforms such as PitchBook and CB Insights are not Phase 1 competitors. They are later reference points only.

---

## 2. Differentiation

Harness wins if it can provide:

- Korean-language interpretation of Physical AI / AGI signals
- high-signal curation from English sources
- plain-language explanation for non-expert Korean readers
- consistent weekly cadence powered by LLM automation
- human editorial gate from President and Vice President
- optional deeper memo once reader trust exists
