## Publicly Observed Positioning

ARK publishes thematic research around disruptive innovation, including AI, robotics, autonomous mobility, digital assets, and related investment themes.

Public reports are visually structured and aimed at readers who want investment-relevant thematic understanding.

---

## What Customers Are Buying Or Following

Even when the report is free, readers use it for:

- thematic investment narrative
- charts and market sizing
- future scenarios
- list of technologies/companies to track
- simplified explanation of complex innovation cycles

---

## Benchmark Lesson For Harness

Harness should learn the report grammar:

- simple thesis
- visual market map
- "why now" timing
- company/technology watchlist
- risk and counter-thesis
- trackable indicators

But Harness must avoid unregistered investment advice.

The safe posture is:

> "Technology and company tracking for education, not buy/sell recommendations."
