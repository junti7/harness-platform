## Publicly Observed Positioning

CB Insights is an enterprise technology intelligence platform.

Public positioning emphasizes:

- market maps
- company data
- buyer/scouting workflows
- AI summaries and briefs
- personalized monitoring
- enterprise team collaboration

---

## What Customers Are Buying

Customers are buying:

- faster discovery of companies and markets
- evidence-backed technology scouting
- reduction of strategic blind spots
- internal workflow support for innovation, M&A, partnership, and investment teams

---

## Benchmark Lesson For Harness

Harness should not copy enterprise scale.

But Harness can copy the job-to-be-done:

- "Tell me what I should track."
- "Tell me which companies matter."
- "Tell me why this matters to my personal objective."
- "Remember what I care about and update me only when it changes."

The personalization layer is crucial for anonymous paid subscribers.
