## Publicly Observed Positioning

PitchBook is a private capital market data and research platform.

Public positioning emphasizes:

- private/public company data
- deal, fund, investor, and market data
- institutional workflows for VC, PE, corporate development, and investment banking
- research reports and market analysis

---

## What Customers Are Buying

Customers are buying:

- structured data
- comparables
- market context
- faster diligence
- confidence in capital allocation workflows

---

## Benchmark Lesson For Harness

Harness cannot compete on data volume.

Harness can compete on:

- narrow domain focus
- Korean explanation
- personalized watchlists
- "why this matters now" interpretation
- small but high-quality manually verified company/technology trackers

Every paid issue should leave behind structured data in the internal Library.
