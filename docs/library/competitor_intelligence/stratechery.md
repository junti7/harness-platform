## Publicly Observed Positioning

Stratechery is a benchmark for solo/creator analyst subscription.

Public positioning emphasizes:

- strategy and business side of technology
- free weekly article plus subscriber-only daily updates
- durable framework-driven analysis rather than link aggregation
- individual and group subscriptions

Public membership page observed:

- Annual subscription: $150/year
- Monthly subscription: $15/month
- Daily Update email/web access/podcast/RSS for subscribers

---

## What Customers Are Buying

Customers are buying:

- one trusted analyst's framework
- repeated interpretation of events through a coherent worldview
- time saved from reading fragmented tech news
- strategic judgment rather than technical completeness

---

## Benchmark Lesson For Harness

Harness needs a recognizable analytical lens.

Possible Harness lens:

> "Physical AI matters only when it changes cost, labor, capacity, safety, or strategic control."

Every issue should answer:

1. What changed?
2. Why does this change the economics?
3. Who benefits?
4. Who is exposed?
5. What should the reader track next?

This is different from "five interesting signals."
