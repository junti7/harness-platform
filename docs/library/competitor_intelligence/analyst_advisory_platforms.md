## Publicly Observed Positioning

Gartner and Forrester sell analyst frameworks, vendor evaluation, and executive decision support.

AlphaSense and Tegus-type platforms sell research retrieval, transcripts, expert insights, and AI-assisted evidence discovery.

---

## What Customers Are Buying

Customers are buying:

- decision support
- evidence retrieval
- vendor/company comparisons
- confidence before budget allocation
- a system that remembers questions and reduces repeated research time

---

## Benchmark Lesson For Harness

Harness should move from generic newsletter to subscriber memory.

Paid product should maintain:

- reader profile
- reader interest tags
- watchlist companies
- watchlist technologies
- prior feedback and confusion points
- preferred depth level
- personal weekly "why this matters to you" section

This is how trust compounds with each issue.
