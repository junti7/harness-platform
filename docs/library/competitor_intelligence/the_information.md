## Publicly Observed Positioning

The Information sells premium technology journalism, newsletters, databases, org charts, events, and professional tools.

Public product surfaces include:

- original reporting
- specialized newsletters
- company org charts
- databases
- pro / enterprise offerings

---

## What Customers Are Buying

Customers are buying:

- access to information not easily available elsewhere
- professional context around companies, people, funding, and strategy
- databases that support repeated work, not one-time reading
- trust in editorial quality and sourcing

---

## Benchmark Lesson For Harness

If Harness remains a weekly PDF only, it is weak.

Paid value should compound into:

- company tracker
- technology tracker
- Korean implication tracker
- reader-specific watchlist
- saved questions and prior recommendations

Personalization and archive memory are part of the product, not an add-on.
